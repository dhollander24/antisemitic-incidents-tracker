Mission Statement

About Me

My name is Delilah Hollander. I created this tracker out of a personal need to understand and document the rising tide of antisemitic incidents happening across America. Over the past few years, I've watched the numbers climb, and the impact has been profound - both on our communities and on me personally.

Why I Built This

I saw antisemitism becoming increasingly visible and violent. News stories about hate crimes, harassment, and discrimination against Jewish people seemed to multiply daily. I realized there wasn't a centralized, accessible place to track and understand the scope of what was happening. So I decided to build one.

What This Tracker Does

This tracker aggregates news reports of antisemitic incidents from across the United States - physical assaults, harassment, vandalism, threats, and discrimination. By documenting these incidents and identifying perpetrators, I hope to provide visibility into a problem that affects millions of Jewish Americans every day.

My Commitment

I am committed to accuracy, transparency, and lifting up the voices of those affected by antisemitism. This tracker is my way of saying: we see what's happening, we're documenting it, and we refuse to let these incidents fade into silence. If you've been affected by antisemitism, or if you see incidents that should be documented, please reach out to antisemiticincidentstracker@gmail.com to help me keep this database current and accurate.

If You Find an Issue

If you notice an incident that's incorrectly categorized, a perpetrator name that's wrong, or information that needs updating, please contact me at antisemiticincidentstracker@gmail.com. This tracker is maintained by one person and your feedback helps keep it accurate and useful for everyone.
