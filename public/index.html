Mission Statement

Our Purpose

The Antisemitic Incidents Tracker is dedicated to monitoring, documenting, and raising awareness about antisemitic acts occurring across the United States. By aggregating news data and tracking incidents, we aim to provide transparency and accountability in the fight against hate and discrimination.

What We Track

We document antisemitic incidents including physical assaults, harassment, threats, vandalism, and discriminatory actions targeting Jewish communities. Our database includes verified incidents from reputable news sources.

Verification Process

All incidents are categorized as "Verified," "Pending Review," or "Disputed" to maintain accuracy and credibility. We encourage community members to help verify information.

Our Commitment

We are committed to accuracy, transparency, and supporting Jewish communities. This tracker serves as a tool for awareness and advocacy against antisemitism.
